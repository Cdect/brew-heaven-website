About Us

A neighbourhood café built around good coffee and good company

Brew Heaven Coffee House brings together great coffee, fresh food, and a welcoming atmosphere where every visit feels special. Tucked near SVVN School on Gudahatti Road, it's a spot for slow mornings, catch-ups with friends, quiet work sessions, and everything in between.

Our beans are roasted for flavour, our kitchen keeps things fresh and simple, and our seating is arranged so you can stay as long as you like — whether that's a quick filter coffee on the way to work or a long afternoon with a book.
